Crystal Collector Game




The computer will generate a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the computer's random number. You will lose if your score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.

PRESS ENTER TO BEGIN!
